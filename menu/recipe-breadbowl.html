Bread Bowls

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups very warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 Tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Pour the very warm water into a bowl. Add the yeast and sugar and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Mix with dough hook on hand-held or stand mixer on low. Then add in the flour and salt. Add olive oil and keep mixing until the dough comes together into a ball. (Knead by hand if no dough hook is available.)
  3. Place dough in a large greased bowl. Cover and let rise for one hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Divide the dough into 3 pieces. Roll each piece until it is a smooth ball. Roll sides under so that the ball is smooth. Place each ball onto a baking sheet covered with parchment paper and cover. Let rise for 30 minutes.
  5. Spoon batter evenly into cupcake wells. Bake for 20 to 22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Preheat the oven to 400F or 200C. Mix together the egg white and water and brush each ball of dough with the egg wash mixture. Bake for about 25 minutes.
  7. Once cool, use a knife to cut a round piece from the top and remove as much of the filling as desired to create the bread bowl. Fill with soup and enjoy!
